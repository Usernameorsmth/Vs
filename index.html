Facts about cats

Unlike dogs, cats do not have a sweet tooth.
When a cat chases its prey, it keeps its head level.
A group of cats is called a “clowder”.
Cats make about 100 different sounds.
Cats have over 30 muscles that control their ears.

Cats are always pushed away due to their "attitude" assumed by humans.
However, they are not as bad as they think! I'm sure you cat owners understand! If YOU don't then it's like assuming all bull or hound dogs are aggressive. But really it's just about the way you bring them up.
